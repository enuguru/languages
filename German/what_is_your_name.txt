

what is your name ? -   wie heißt du ?   (this is informal)

du is an informal version of you

What is your name ? -   Wie heisen Sie ?  (this is formal)

Sie is an informal version of you 

What is your name ? -   Wie ist Ihr Name ? (this is very formal)


and the answer is 
my name is Guru     -    Mein Name ist Guru
or
I am Guru           -    Ich bin Guru
or 
I am called Guru    -    Ich heiße Guru  
and you ?           -    Und du ? (informal)
and you ?           -    Und Sie ? (formal)

heißt and heiße both are forms of the verb heißen which means 
"to be called"



you can also ask like this in German - 

who are you ?       -    Wer Sind Sie ?  ( this is formally)
(this question looks slightly rude in English, but in German it is ok
especially when asked with the proper tone)

who are you ?       -    Wer bist du ? (this is informal)
