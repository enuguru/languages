
where are you from ?


where are you from    -     Woher Kommen Sie ?   (formal way of doing)

Wo      -   where
her     -   from
Kommen  -   to come
Sie     -   formal way of saying you

where are you from    -     Woher kommst du ? (informal - asking )    

or

where are you from   -      Wo kommst du ?    (informal - asking)

I am from India
Ich komme aus Indien

Ich komme aus China
