
occupation
----------


occupation in German is der Beruf

chef - der koch (pronounced as kof) for male and die Köchin (kochen)

doctor -  der Arzt for male and die Ärztin

engineer - der Ingenieur for male and die Ingenieurin


what do you do for a living ? - was macht du beruflich?

the answer would be I am an engineer -  Ich bin Ingenieur

farmer  -  die Bauer for male farmer and die Bäuerin

pilot   -  der Pilot for male pilot and die Piloten

waiter  -  der Kellner for male waiter and die Kellnerin
