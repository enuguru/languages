

how are you ?
-------------

informal way of asking someone

how are you - comment ca va

fine, thank you - ca va bien merci

not well    - ca va mal

not bad    -  pas mal

neither good nor bad - comment ci comme ca



formal way of asking someone

how are you - comment allez vous

i am fine thank you - Je vais bien merci

and you (informal) - et toi  -  

and you  (formal)  - et vous
